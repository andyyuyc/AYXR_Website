<div class="about-section">
  <div class="section-head">
    <span class="section-num">01</span>
    <h2 class="section-title">// what we build</h2>
  </div>
  <div class="two-col">
    <div>
      <h3 class="col-title">Virtual Reality</h3>
      <p>We design and develop spatial software for modern XR platforms — Apple Vision Pro, Meta Quest, and the wider ecosystem. Our work spans immersive applications, spatial interaction patterns, and tooling that helps creators reach into 3D space without friction.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3 class="col-title">iOS Applications</h3>
      <p>We ship native iOS software written in Swift and SwiftUI. We're drawn to apps that feel calm, fast, and inevitable — the kind of product that quietly becomes part of someone's day. We build for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and visionOS.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="about-section">
  <div class="section-head">
    <span class="section-num">02</span>
    <h2 class="section-title">// how we work</h2>
  </div>
  <ul class="num-list">
    <li><span class="num">01</span><span><strong>Single thread.</strong> One project gets our full attention. No context-switching tax, no half-finished work in the drawer.</span></li>
    <li><span class="num">02</span><span><strong>Prototype first.</strong> We ship a working slice in weeks, not months. The shortest path to a real artifact is almost always the right one.</span></li>
    <li><span class="num">03</span><span><strong>Detail compounds.</strong> A great app is a thousand small decisions made in the same direction. We don't skip the small ones.</span></li>
    <li><span class="num">04</span><span><strong>Code is craft.</strong> We write code we'd be happy to read on a Monday morning. So can you.</span></li>
  </ul>
</div>
